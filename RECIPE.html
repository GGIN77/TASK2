Delicious Pasta Recipe

A quick and tasty pasta dish you can make at home

Ingredients

  • 200g Pasta
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 2 Garlic Cloves (minced)
  • 1 Can Tomato Sauce
  • 1 tsp Oregano
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • Grated Cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté onion and garlic until golden.
  3. Add tomato sauce, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Mix pasta with the sauce and stir well.
  5. Serve hot with grated cheese on top.
